# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want SMBD to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless
of experience, identity, or background. We pledge to act and interact in ways
that make participation a positive experience.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps:

- Being respectful, kind, and constructive.
- Welcoming newcomers and differing viewpoints.
- Giving and gracefully accepting feedback.
- Focusing on what's best for the project and community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments; personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Using SMBD or its community to facilitate harassment, doxxing, mass-reporting,
  or surveillance of individuals. (SMBD is a transparency tool — outputs are
  probabilistic signals, not accusations. See [docs/faq.md](docs/faq.md).)
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove, edit, or
reject contributions, comments, commits, and issues that violate this Code, and
may temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviors they deem
inappropriate.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainers via the repository's
**Security → Report a vulnerability** channel or by contacting a maintainer
directly. All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.
